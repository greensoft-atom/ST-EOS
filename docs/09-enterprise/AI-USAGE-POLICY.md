# ST-EOS AI Usage Policy (Pilot)

**Document ID:** AI-USAGE-POLICY  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-06-29  
**Requires:** Executive sponsor approval before pilot

---

## 1. Purpose

Define acceptable use of ST-EOS AI features for pilot institution staff.

---

## 2. Policy Statements

1. **Advisory only** — AI outputs do not constitute official decisions, legal opinions, or patentability determinations.
2. **Human authority** — Evaluation scores, approved reports, and official documents require human review and sign-off.
3. **Verify citations** — Users must check source documents before relying on factual claims.
4. **Classification required** — Do not upload CONFIDENTIAL documents without curator approval.
5. **No bypass attempts** — Users must not attempt to circumvent access controls via prompts.
6. **Report issues** — Incorrect or harmful outputs must be reported to administrators.

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

| Prohibited | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| Final grant decisions without committee review | Accountability |
| Legal/patent advice as authoritative | Liability |
| Uploading personal unrelated data | Privacy |
| Sharing login credentials | Security |
| Automated bulk scraping of restricted content | Policy |

---

## 4. User Acknowledgment

Before first AI use, users click:

> "I understand ST-EOS AI is advisory, may contain errors, and requires my verification."
